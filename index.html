SoulCoast Pulse Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how SoulCoast Pulse ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you register for an account, we collect your email address, name, and profile information.
  • User Content: Any content you voluntarily provide within the App, including:
    • Profile information and photos
    • Posts, comments, and messages in the community feed
    • Event attendance and registrations
    • Feedback on classes, events, and journeys
    • Book club discussions and comments
    • Art night submissions and comments
  • Device Information: Information about your device, including device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and IP address.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, including features accessed, time spent, interactions, navigation patterns, and content preferences.
  • Photos and Media: When you grant permissions, we access your camera and photo library to allow you to upload profile pictures, event images, art night submissions, and other content.
  • Location Data: With your permission, approximate location data to enhance features like event discovery and community connections.
  • Communication Data: Correspondence and messages sent within the App, including dance invitations and direct messages.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality
  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your profile and content to other community members
  • Process your requests, transactions, and class/event registrations
  • Enable community features including the feed, comments, and messaging
  • Facilitate event management and dance class scheduling
  • Support book club discussions and journey explorations
  • Allow art night submissions and speaker engagements
  • Communicate with you about the App and community activities
  • Understand how users interact with our App to improve user experience
  • Personalize your experience based on your preferences
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

Community Content and Public Information

The App includes community features where users can share content publicly. Please note:

  • Content posted to the community feed, book club discussions, art night gallery, and public event comments may be visible to all App users
  • Your profile information and activity may be visible to other community members
  • Public content may persist even after you delete your account
  • Exercise caution when sharing personal information in public areas of the App

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Other Users: Content you post in public areas of the App will be visible to other users according to your privacy settings.
  • With Your Consent: We may share information when you direct us to do so.
  • Service Providers: We work with third-party service providers to perform services on our behalf, including hosting, analytics, authentication, and cloud storage.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.
  • Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: For authentication, data storage, analytics, and cloud functions.
  • Google Sign-In: For authentication services.
  • Expo: For app development and distribution tools.

Each third-party service may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the services you have requested, or for other essential purposes such as complying with legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing our agreements. User-generated content in public areas of the App may remain even after you delete your account.

TestFlight Data Collection

When using the TestFlight version of our App, please be aware that:

  • Crash logs and usage statistics are automatically collected and shared with both Apple and our development team.
  • Device information including hardware specifications, operating system version, performance data, battery level, and screen resolution is collected.
  • App uptime, free disk space, network connection type, and time zone information may be collected.
  • Any feedback, screenshots, or comments you submit through TestFlight will be accessible to our development team.
  • Apple retains beta app feedback for one year and may retain crash log and usage data until bugs are resolved.

Your Rights and Choices

You can control certain data collection and use:

  • Account Information: Update or correct your account information in your profile settings.
  • App Permissions: Control app permissions such as camera, photo access, and location through your device settings.
  • Communications: Opt out of marketing communications by following unsubscribe instructions.
  • Content Deletion: Delete specific content you've posted in the community, though copies may exist in cached or archived pages.
  • Account Deletion: Request deletion of your account by contacting us. Some information may persist due to legal, legitimate business, or technical requirements.
  • Do Not Track: We do not currently respond to Do Not Track signals.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including encryption, secure server protocols, and access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13 years of age (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using the App, you consent to these transfers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page, updating the "Last Updated" date, and, where appropriate, notifying you through the App or via email.
